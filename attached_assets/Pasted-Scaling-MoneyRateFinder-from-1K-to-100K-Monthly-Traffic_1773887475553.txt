When we launched MoneyRateFinder, we weren’t naïve.

🧠 Internal Metric: Intent Score (0–100)

We created a scoring system:

Intent Score = (Specificity × 0.4) + (Transaction Signal × 0.4) + (Urgency × 0.2)
